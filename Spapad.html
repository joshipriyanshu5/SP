Years of Excellence

Born in the Blue City, Loved Worldwide

Samvidha Papad is more than just a food product — it's a celebration of Rajasthan's rich culinary heritage. Nestled in the historic city of Jodhpur, known worldwide as the "Blue City," our family has been perfecting the art of papad-making for over two decades.

Every papad we create carries the essence of the Thar Desert's golden sands, the warmth of the Rajasthani sun, and the time-honored techniques passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

Our commitment to quality means using only the finest ingredients — premium lentils, aromatic spices sourced directly from local markets, and pure oil. Each batch is hand-rolled by expert craftsmen who understand that great papad requires patience, precision, and passion.
